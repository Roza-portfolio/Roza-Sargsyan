BIOGRAPHY

Roza Sargsyan graduated from the Yerevan State Institute of Theatre and Cinema as a Set Designer. Her other professional specializations include Miniature Artist, Costume Designer, Production Designer, and Props Artist.

DISCIPLINES

EDUCATION

THE PHILOSOPHY

"Every object on stage carries the weight of a story. My work is about finding the truth in materials, the poetry in space, and the silent dialogue between a character and their world."
